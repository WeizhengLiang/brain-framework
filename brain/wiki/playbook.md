# Playbook

Living document. Rules here are distilled from [[retrospectives]] — patterns that proved themselves across multiple tasks. Each rule earned its place through evidence, not theory.

## How to use

- Read this at session start alongside CLAUDE.md
- Rules are tagged with confidence: `proven` (3+ confirming retros), `emerging` (1-2 retros), `questioned` (contradicted by recent evidence)
- When a rule consistently proves itself, propose graduating it to CLAUDE.md
- When a rule stops being relevant, archive it with a note on why
